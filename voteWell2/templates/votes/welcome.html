Welcome to Votewell.info

What is votewell.info

Votewell.info is social networking tool for keeping track of our legislators. There's so much information out there about Congress, it can be hard to wade through. Votewell.info points you to the votes that matter, by showing you the votes that people are talking about. And it lets you highlight the votes that you care about, to help others stay informed. So whether you're a policy wonk looking to turn the spotlight on what's really happening in Congress, or a first time voter just looking for some information, votewell.info is for you.
